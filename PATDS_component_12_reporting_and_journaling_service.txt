# 🟦 Component #12 — Reporting & Journaling Service  
This is where PATDS becomes *reflective*. Up to now, your system has been capturing, transcribing, enriching, indexing, and organizing your life. PATDS begins **telling the story of your days, weeks, and months** — automatically.

This service generates **daily summaries**, **weekly reviews**, **monthly reflections**, **meeting reports**, and **custom journals** using all the structured data PATDS looks through.
This service transforms your structured data into **narratives**, **summaries**, and **reports** that help you understand your life at a glance.

It’s the “reflection engine” of PATDS.

---

# 🧩 Subcomponents

## 1. **Daily Summary Generator**  
This is the most frequently used report.

### Inputs  
- Timeline entries  
- Processed transcripts  
- Tasks created/completed  
- Topics & entities  
- Sentiment (optional)

### Output (Markdown or HTML)  
```
# Daily Summary — June 26, 2026

## Overview
You discussed project planning, scheduled a meeting with Alex, and created three new tasks.

## Key Actions
- Send the proposal (due tomorrow)
- Schedule meeting with Alex (next week)

## Conversations
- 14:03 — Discussion about project timeline
- 16:20 — Planning next week’s tasks

## Notes
- “Remember to send the proposal”
```

---

## 2. **Weekly Review Generator**  
### Highlights  
- Top topics of the week  
- People you interacted with most  
- Tasks completed vs created  
- Mood trends (if sentiment enabled)  
- Most important conversations  

---

## 3. **Monthly Reflection Generator**  
### Includes  
- Major themes  
- Accomplishments  
- Challenges  
- Recurring patterns  
- Suggested goals for next month  

---

## 4. **Meeting Report Generator**  
Triggered when a transcript is tagged as a meeting.

### Output  
- Attendees  
- Agenda (inferred)  
- Key decisions  
- Action items  
- Follow‑ups  
- Summary  

---

## 5. **Custom Journaling Engine**  
Allows the user to define templates like:

- “Gratitude journal”  
- “Work log”  
- “Health log”  
- “Daily reflection”  

PATDS fills in the structured parts and leaves space for free‑text.

---

## 6. **Report Scheduler**  
### MVP  
- Generate daily summary at midnight  
- Generate weekly review on Sunday  
- Generate monthly reflection on the 1st  

### Advanced  
- User‑defined schedules  
- Triggered reports (“After every meeting…”)  

---

## 7. **Report Storage**  
Stored in the `documents` collection with type:

- `daily_summary`  
- `weekly_review`  
- `monthly_reflection`  
- `meeting_report`  
- `custom_journal`  

---

## 8. **Report API**  
### Endpoints  
- `POST /report/generate?type=daily_summary&date=...`  
- `GET /report/:id`  
- `GET /reports?type=weekly_review`  

---

## 9. **UI Integration**  
### Mobile

- Weekly review carousel  
- Journaling prompts  

### Web  
- Full report viewer  
- Export options
